As of 2026-05-08, the project has pivoted to **Powerbird** — a persistent drone that autonomously harvests energy from power lines via magnetic induction.

**Why:** New concept, completely different product and market from Chargebotic.

**Powerbird concept:**
- Drone latches onto high-voltage power lines
- Harvests energy via induction coil (non-contact)
- Power converter → DC battery recharge
- Autonomous cycle: Find Line → Land & Connect → Harvest Energy → Take Off
- Enables unlimited flight endurance with zero ground recharges

**Use cases:** Line inspection, wildfire monitoring, comms relay, border surveillance, renewables monitoring

**Website:** Rebuilt from scratch at chargebotic.com (Vercel) — new brand, new CSS (powerbird.css), same deployment

**How to apply:** All prior Chargebotic positioning, products, and content are no longer relevant to this project. The team (Anis + Christopher) remains the same.
