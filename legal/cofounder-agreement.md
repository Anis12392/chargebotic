> **Guidance:** YC recommends near-equal splits (50/50) because "all the work is ahead of you." A 51/49 split gives the CEO tiebreaking authority while remaining fair. A 10-15% option pool is standard at pre-seed.
>
> **Common options:**
> - **50/50** — Equal partnership, requires deadlock resolution mechanism
> - **51/49** — Near-equal, CEO has tiebreak, preferred by some VCs
> - **60/40** — If one founder has significantly more prior contribution (IP, capital, traction)

## 7. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

**7.1 Non-Compete:** During the term of this Agreement and for **12 months** following departure, neither Co-Founder shall directly or indirectly engage in any business that competes with the Company's core business (autonomous robot charging solutions).

> **Note:** California law generally does not enforce non-competes against employees. However, this clause is included as a statement of intent and may be enforceable in other jurisdictions or in the context of a stock sale/acquisition. Consult counsel.

**7.2 Non-Solicitation:** For **12 months** following departure, neither Co-Founder shall solicit or hire any employee, contractor, or consultant of the Company, or solicit any customer, partner, or supplier of the Company.

## 11. DEPARTURE OF A CO-FOUNDER

**11.1 Voluntary Departure:** A Co-Founder who voluntarily leaves the Company forfeits all **unvested** shares. Vested shares are retained.

**11.2 Termination for Cause:** If a Co-Founder is removed for Cause (defined below), they forfeit all **unvested** shares. The Company shall have the right (but not obligation) to repurchase **vested** shares at Fair Market Value.

**11.3 Definition of Cause:**
- Material breach of this Agreement
- Conviction of a felony
- Willful misconduct or gross negligence
- Fraud, embezzlement, or misappropriation of Company assets
- Persistent failure to perform duties after written notice and 30-day cure period

**11.4 Disability or Death:** If a Co-Founder becomes permanently disabled or dies, all shares shall **immediately vest** in full. The Company (or surviving Co-Founder) shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the shares at Fair Market Value.
